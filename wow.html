World of Warcraft

Raiding progression, heroic requirements, and signup sheet.

Normal Raid

Normal raids are open to all members. At the beginning of each season, we learn the fights on normal and progress through the raid until most players no longer need normal boss loot.

Heroic Raid

Heroic raids are for experienced players who are competent with raid mechanics and their roles on the team. Each season, we complete the Heroic raid and earn AOTC as a guild.

Normal Raid, Cont.

Once we move on to Heroic progression, we often dedicate a second night to continue farming Normal raid. Alts and new players are welcome to join us in a more casual setting.

Heroic Raiding Requirements

Mechanics

Knows where to stand, what boss moves look like, etc. Still alive at the end of most fights.

Role

Damaging / healing numbers at least half of the top average. Uses raid cooldowns on cue.

Attitude

Keeps a cool head during progression. Constructive feedback given and accepted.

Gear

Item level higher than normal raid loot. Completes weekly content and upgrades.

Build

Talented in the right abilities. Aware of best-in-slot drops from the raid loot table.

Consumables

Uses weapon buffs, food buffs, flasks, etc. Keeps gems and enchants current.
