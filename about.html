About Us

Who we are, and what we're all about.

Our History

Our group was established by a handful of WoW players who continued playing together through multiple expansions, different game trends, and life changes. We've been together since 2010, and we're still going strong!

The Community

Our members are primarily adults who make time for gaming despite our busy schedules. Since we only have limited time to spend together, we make sure that real-world drama doesn't intrude in our conversations.

Group Activities

While most of us play WoW together, we also host a number of other gaming opportunities. Many of us play MTG, and we often launch private gaming servers that have included Rust, Abiotic Factor, 7 Days, Enshrouded, etc.
